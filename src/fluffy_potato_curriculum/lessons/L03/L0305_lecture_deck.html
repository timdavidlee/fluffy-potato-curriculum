The through-line

  • An explicit, narrow step is the unit worth orchestrating.
  • The ceremony exists to make one LLM call reusable and inspectable instead of a one-off.

Naming the step plainly is what gives you a seam to test, compose, swap, and see.

Slide 3.1

One node: not obviously worth it

  • For a single node the ceremony isn't obviously worth it over a plain function — await extract_app.ainvoke(...) is more setup than extract_plain(...).
  • Discipline gets an inline version partway. The difference: a typed state schema makes the seam enforced by structure, not a convention you keep up.
  • The value shows up at the second step — which is exactly the next lesson.
