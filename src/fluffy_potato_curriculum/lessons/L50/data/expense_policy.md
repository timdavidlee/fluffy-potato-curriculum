# Acme Corp — Expense Reimbursement Policy

We reimburse reasonable, business-necessary expenses. Use good judgment: when a
receipt sits near a limit or the category is unclear, favor flagging it for a human
over guessing.

## Meals

Individual meals while travelling are reimbursed up to **$50 per person**. Team
meals and client entertainment may exceed this with a manager's note on the report.

## Travel (ground transport)

Taxis, rideshares, and transit are reimbursed up to **$75 per trip**. Airport
transfers and late-night trips (after 9pm) are exempt from the cap.

## Lodging

Hotels are reimbursed up to **$250 per night**, taxes included. Conference-block
rates and high-cost-city stays above this need prior approval and should be flagged.

## Supplies

Office and project supplies are reimbursed up to **$150** per purchase. Anything
that reads as equipment (over $150) goes through Procurement, not expenses.

## Not reimbursable

Alcohol, personal entertainment, and gifts over $25 are not reimbursed. If a
category is not covered above, do not guess — send it for manual review.
